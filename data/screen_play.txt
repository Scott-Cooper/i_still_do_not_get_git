TITLE: PERFORMANCE REVIEW
BY: SCOTT COOPER

INT. OFFICE – DAY 1

A beige, windowless office. Fluorescent lights hum faintly overhead. A wall clock ticks too loudly.

KEVIN sits behind a desk. CHAD sits across from him.
They stare at each other.
Silence.
The clock ticks.
KEVIN blinks. Immediately regrets it.
CHAD narrows his eyes slightly. Not aggressive—just… present.
Silence stretches.
KEVIN shifts in his chair. The chair squeaks. It echoes.
CHAD’s gaze follows the movement. He does not blink.
KEVIN swallows. Audible.
CHAD tilts his head. Just a few degrees.
KEVIN tries to mirror it. Stops halfway. Returns upright.
Silence.
The fluorescent light flickers once.
Both men notice.
Neither acknowledges it.
The clock ticks louder. Or maybe it’s just them.
KEVIN inhales like he’s about to speak.
He doesn’t.
CHAD inhales too. Slightly later. Slightly louder.
They both exhale. Out of sync.
Silence.
KEVIN’s eye twitches.
CHAD notices. His own eye twitches in response. Or coincidence.
KEVIN leans forward an inch.
CHAD leans forward an inch.
They are now marginally closer. It feels catastrophic.
Silence.
KEVIN places his hands on the desk.
CHAD slowly places his hands on his own knees.
KEVIN glances at CHAD’s hands.
CHAD notices the glance.
KEVIN returns to eye contact too quickly.
Silence.
The clock ticks.
KEVIN blinks twice in quick succession.
CHAD blinks once. Deliberate. Controlled.
KEVIN’s lips part.
A breath escapes.
CHAD’s lips part.
Nothing escapes.
Silence.
KEVIN’s chair creaks again as he subtly adjusts.
CHAD’s chair does not creak. It simply absorbs him.
KEVIN looks briefly at the clock.
CHAD watches KEVIN look at the clock.
KEVIN looks back at CHAD.
Too fast again.
Silence.
CHAD’s foot taps once.
It is very loud.
CHAD freezes his foot mid-air, then slowly lowers it.
KEVIN’s eyes flick down to the foot, then back up.
They lock eyes again.
Silence.
Silence.
The clock ticks...

CUT TO BLACK
